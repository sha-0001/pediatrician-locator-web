### HOLIDAY OPERATIONS

During National Holidays:
- **All 24/7 Hospitals:** Continue 24-hour operations
- **Specialist Clinics:** Typically CLOSED - Use hospital emergency services
- **Recommendation:** For holiday pediatric emergencies, go directly to nearest 24-hour hospital

### EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

**For Pediatric Emergencies (Nights, Weekends, Holidays):**
1. **Immediate Access:**
   - Go to nearest 24-hour hospital (St. Elizabeth, Sarangani Bay, Mindanao Medical Center, or Dadiangas)
   - Call (083) 887-8888 (Sarangani Bay) for ambulance coordination
   - Call (083) 552-3162 (St. Elizabeth) for emergency guidance

2. **Life-Threatening Situations:**
   - All 24-hour hospitals have pediatric emergency departments
   - Bring insurance/identification documents
   - Have recent medical records available if possible
