About Me

ℹ️ The Story

Pediatrician Locator Website started as a simple idea to help locate the nearest pediatricians from the user's location.

Google Maps can already locate pediatricians, but this website aims to make the experience easier for parents or older people who have difficulty using maps. Once the system gets their location, it automatically pinpoints where they are and finds the nearest clinic or hospital.
